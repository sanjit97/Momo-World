ABOUT US

OVERVIEW ACHIEVEMENTS FACILITIES LOCATION

Momo world is a chain of fast food restaurants headquartered in New Delhi, specializing in Momos, a dumpling popular in Nepal and parts of Tibet and India. We are spread across the city to Chennai, Pune, Bengaluru, Kochi, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bhubaneshwar. Over the years, we have grown rapidly to become the country's largest momo chain in terms of volumes, turnovers & innovations. Our menu caters different flavors of momos available in steamed, fried and pan fried formats largely in white and brown flour variants. We are smartly innovated and invented to create Sizzler Momos, MoBurg (Momo Burgers), Momo Chats etc. And to top it all, even our dessert is a Chocolate momo. We operate out of Kiosks, Food Court Outlets, High street mini outlet, and High Street maxi outlets.
